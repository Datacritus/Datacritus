Welcome to DataCritus

Inspired by the ancient Greek philosopher Democritus, one of the first to propose that the universe is governed by natural laws and logic, DataCritus aims to bring rational analysis to modern governance. Our platform breaks down complex political performance into measurable data points (KPIs), giving citizens the tools to compare, evaluate, and hold governments accountable. Because, as Democritus once said: "Everything happens by necessity and reason."
